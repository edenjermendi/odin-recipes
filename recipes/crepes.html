Hungarian Crepes Recipe

Description

Hungarian pancakes are thin crepes, rolled up and sprinkled with powdered sugar.

It's a quick dessert that's done in under 30 minutes and tastes amazing.

Ingredients

    Batter:

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 2/3 cup sparkling water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Oil for frying

    • Filling:

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2/3 cup dry cottage cheese or ricotta
  • 1/2 lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Steps

  1. Beat eggs, salt, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Add flour and milk gradually to avoid lumps. Mix well.
  3. Add remaining flour, milk, and sparkling water. Rest 5-10 min.
  4. Meanwhile, mash cottage cheese and combine with sugar, egg yolk, vanilla, and lemon zest.
  5. Heat pan with oil. Pour in batter, swirl to coat. Fry each side until golden.
  6. Fill with jam or cheese mixture. Roll and dust with icing sugar. Serve warm!
