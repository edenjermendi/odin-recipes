Chicken Pasta Bake Recipe

Description

This delicious comfort meal, a hearty pasta bake is layered with tomato sauce and cream, a variety of melted cheeses, and chicken and mushrooms (or your choice of meats/veges).

It's easy to prepare, great for leftovers, and ideal for cozy dinners or meal prep throughout the week.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken thighs or breasts (season to taste)
  • 300g Pasta (I use rigatoni or pene) and water for the pasta
  • 1/2 onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Bacon (optional but yum)
  • Pasta bake jar or tomato passata and cream (measure with heart)
  • Spinach, broccoli, mushrooms and capsicum
  • Mozarella, parmesan, cheddar and/or even feta!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C
  2. Cook pasta for a couple minutes less than instructions say
  3. Season chicken and let sit for now
  4. Cirsp bacon and use the remaining fat for the chicken and veges
  5. Cook onions and garlic now, add extra oil if needed
  6. Sizzle chicken, then mushrooms and other veges for a few minutes
  7. Then add sauce and remaining ingredients
  8. Add pasta in batches so your pan doesn't spill over
  9. Add to heatproof dish, grate heaps of cheese to your liking
  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until cheese melted with golden spots
  11. Stand for 5 minutes then serve!
