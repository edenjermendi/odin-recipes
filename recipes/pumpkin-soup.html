Pumpkin Soup

Description

A creamy, comforting soup made with fresh pumpkin, aromatics, and a splash of cream. Simple to make and deeply satisfying.

Perfect with crusty bread for chilly evenings or cozy lunches.

Ingredients

  • ~ 1kg pumpkin
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 cups vegetable or chicken broth/stock
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper

    • To finish:

  • 1/2 - 3/4 cup cream

Steps

  1. Cut the pumpkin into 3cm slices. Remove skin and seeds, and chop into chunks.
  2. Add pumpkin, onion, garlic, broth, and water to a pot. Bring to boil uncovered, then simmer ~10 min until tender.
  3. Blend until smooth with a stick blender or in batches.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, stir in cream. (Do not boil after adding cream.)
  5. Serve hot, garnished with extra cream, pepper, and parsley. Crusty bread recommended!
